Founder of S.D College

Sanatan Dharma College was establish in 1916 in Lahore under the leadership of the Indian scholar and educational reformer, Bharat Ratna Mahamana Madan Mohan Malviya, ji.
After the partition of the country, the College was re-established in Ambala where the foundation stone was laid by India's first President Dr Rajender Prasad on 3 October 1951.
The college has educated many notable alumni including Dr N Vohra, Governor, J & K; Mrs Sushma Swaraj, Minster of External Affairs; Sh Anil Vij, Minister of Health and Sports, Haryana; and Nobel Laureate and Nuclear Physicist, Dr Abdus Salam.
The college embraces innovative concepts to prepare students for contemporary global challenges and looks forward to cooperating with the OERu in achieving these goals.
The college is one of the premier institution of Western Uttar Pradesh.
It is managed by a registered body viz.
S. D. College Association which also supervises several other technical and educational institutes as well as social welfare associations.
The college enjoys a glorious past, an impressive present and the vision of a very bright future.
‘March Forward’ is not just a slogan here but a time-tested tradition. At present several ambitious projects are on cards and are likely to take shape in near future.
The great height that S. D. College has touched and spectacular progress
it has recorded in various fields are the result of the selfless service and dedication of several luminaries who are no more.
S.D College, Muzaffarnagar is concerned about self-reliant India and follow the mission of Indian Government of “Skill India”.

The college has opened up a Skill Development Centre under the UGC Scheme of Deen Dayal Upadhyay Centre for Knowledge Acquisition and Upgradation of Skilled Human Ability and livelihood (Kaushal)

Our Cources

BCA

BCA is bachelor's in Computer Application. BCA is a 3-year undergraduate degree programme that focusses on knowledge on the basics of computer application and software development. A BCA degree is considered to be at par with a BTech/BE degree in Computer Science or Information Technology.

BBA

BBA is a three-year professional undergraduate course in Business Management. The course is open to students from all three streams i.e., Science, Arts and Commerce. The BBA course offers knowledge and training in management and leadership skills to prepare them for managerial roles and entrepreneurship..

BSc

B.Sc. or Bachelor of Science is an undergraduate degree of three years duration which is universally offered across institutes and universities across India. B. Sc. is one of the most popular courses opted for by students who have a flair for scientific aptitude and zeal for research-oriented.
